# ECD Mapping: Survival Equation — Collaborative Puzzle Survival

> **Status:** DRAFT — To be completed when game development begins.

## Game Overview

- **Mechanic:** 2-4 students stranded in an environment (desert island, space station, underwater base). Each has a unique specialist role with exclusive tools/information. Problems require combining information across roles.
- **Subject:** Math & Science (applied survival problems)
- **Duration:** 15-20 min per scenario
- **Primary Analytics Dimension:** D4 (Social & Collaborative — communication, team contribution, role adoption)

---

## 1. Competency Model

| Competency | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Applied Problem Solving** | Math/science applied to authentic survival contexts (water purification ratios, structural load) |
| **Communication Quality** | On-task vs. off-task messaging; information sharing completeness |
| **Team Contribution Equity** | Even distribution of problem-solving effort across team members |
| **Leadership & Social Initiation** | Who initiates conversations, proposes solutions, coordinates team |
| **Conflict Resolution** | How teams handle disagreements about resource allocation |
| **Empathy** | Sharing scarce resources when not personally beneficial |
